The Notion Collective

The Librarians

Date
December 2, 2013 – January 9, 2014
Venue
Queens College
Location
New York City

In a series of five Untitled works, The Notion Collective explores the changing role of the library in our increasingly digital culture.

The Librarians was exhibited in conjunction with Hawkeye Collective at Queens College Art Center.

Collaborators

  • Jon Wohl
  • Andy Dayton
  • Jason Bahling
