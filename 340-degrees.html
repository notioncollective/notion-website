340 Degrees

Date
February 23, 2011
Venue
MNKINO #18
Location
Minneapolis, MN

340 degrees is the temperature at which sugars begin to caramelize. The process we call caramelization occurs when we heat a variety of foods past this temperature. In fact, caramelization is responsible for much of what we aim for and find delicious in cooking. It's true for nuts, it's true for cane sugar, and it's true for the roasted coffee bean.
