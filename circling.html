The Notion Collective

Circling

Date
February 4, 2013
Venue
Dance On Camera Festival at Lincoln Center (US Premiere)
Location
New York

Circling is a meditation on place, the passing of time and the cyclical nature of life. Featuring renowned choreographer/dancer Sally Gross and shot at locations including architect Frank Lloyd Wright's Taliesin, Circling is a poetic rendering of what it means to be deeply at home in both the landscape and the dancing body. Jason Bahling and Michael Eckblad worked with Douglas Rosenberg and Sally Gross on this production.
